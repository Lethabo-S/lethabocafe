How do I place an order?
Browse the Menu tab, open an item and tap Add to cart, then check out from the Cart tab.
What is the Scan tab for?
Scan the QR code on your table to link your order, or scan a loyalty code to collect Rosé Rewards points.
I forgot my password
Use Reset password from the sign-in screen to receive a reset link by email.
Contact support
Email support@lethabocafe.example and we'll get back to you within a day.
